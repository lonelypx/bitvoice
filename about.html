About Bitvoice

Our combined expertise in varied voice-based technologies will prove to be the formative base for Bit Voice when we aim to partner with clients from different industries and verticals and thus build a good, continuing client base. We deliver client-centric solutions with our ground-breaking communication technology solutions, steadfast focus and assimilating the real-life business context of our clients and all this aided by an experienced team with the knowhow and proficiency. Our capability extends over varied industries and verticals. We believe in nurturing and fostering longstanding client relationships and this is evident in our tactical insights, revolutionary technological solutions and strategized services that we offer our clients to help them gain distinctive benefits.

Our brand name Bit Voice is basically an adaptation of 'the basic of an IP Packet'. If we were to elaborate on what the 'Bit' stands for, we have derived it from the world of telecommunications and computers. Therefore, 'Bit' is from the telecom sphere and when combined with Voice our core business functionality, we have 'Bit Voice', a collaboration of voice, data and video enabled on a single platform.
