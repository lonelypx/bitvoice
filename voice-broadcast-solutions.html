Voice Broadcast Solutions

Voice broadcasting solutions will allow customer to record or upload an audio file (Your own voice clips which recorded through phone or any real-time generated text to voice converted file) and then have it automatically transmitted over phone lines to a list of contacts that you provide.

The different application for the solution will be for the following applications.

  • Emergency voice message delivery to a group of key personnel's in a factory/ industry in case of and unexpected event.
  • Medical emergency voice message delivery to a group of medical team in case of an emergency / Accident at hospital.
  • Remainder call for debt collection
  • Lead generation and telemarketing
  • Mass customer survey
  • Feedback calling
  • Political campaigns

The core Bit Voice Team comprises of three founding members who come with a collective industry-specific experience of over 30 years. Our extensive work experience in the areas of voice-based software design, development and execution which also includes the technical knowhow, pitching and maintenance capabilities worked very well for us when we combined forces under our brand entity, Bit Voice.
